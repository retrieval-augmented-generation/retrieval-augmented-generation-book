# Q3 2025 Pricing Structure

**Effective:** July 1 – September 30, 2025
**Supersedes:** Q2 2025 Pricing Structure
**Approved by:** Lisa Yamamoto, CFO

---

## Standard Pricing

| Tier | Monthly Price (per seat) | Annual Price (per seat) | Minimum Seats |
|---|---|---|---|
| Starter | $19/month | $199/year ($16.58/month effective) | 1 |
| Pro | $59/month | $637/year ($53.08/month effective) | 5 |
| Enterprise | Custom | Custom | 50 |

The Pro tier price of $59/seat/month reflects the adjustment effective November 1, 2024. No further changes to Starter or Pro pricing are planned for Q3 2025.

Enterprise pricing is negotiated per account based on seat count, product lines included, contract length, and support tier. See the Enterprise Pricing Calculator (internal tool) for indicative ranges.

## Volume Discounts

Volume discounts apply to Pro and Enterprise tiers based on committed seat count at contract signing:

| Committed Seats | Pro Discount | Enterprise Discount |
|---|---|---|
| 50–99 | 5% | Included in negotiation |
| 100–249 | 10% | Included in negotiation |
| 250–499 | 15% | Included in negotiation |
| 500+ | 20% | Included in negotiation |

Volume discounts are applied to the annual rate. Monthly-billed customers are not eligible for volume discounts.

The minimum contract value for Enterprise discount eligibility is $50,000 annually. Contracts below this threshold are served on the Pro tier with volume discounts as applicable.

## Product Line Add-Ons

In Q3 2025, the following product lines are available as add-ons to the base platform subscription:

| Add-On | Price | Available On |
|---|---|---|
| Data & Analytics | $15/seat/month | Pro, Enterprise |
| Compliance Suite | $12/seat/month | Pro, Enterprise |
| Advanced API (higher rate limits) | $8/seat/month | All tiers |

Add-on pricing is not subject to volume discounts unless bundled in an Enterprise agreement.

## Contract Terms

- **Starter:** Month-to-month or annual. No minimum commitment beyond 1 seat.
- **Pro:** Month-to-month or annual. Annual contracts receive approximately 10% savings over monthly billing. 5-seat minimum.
- **Enterprise:** Annual or multi-year (2–3 years). Multi-year contracts receive an additional 5–10% discount negotiated by the account team. 50-seat minimum.

## Q3 Promotions

No active promotions for Q3 2025. The Q2 "Migrate from Competitor" promotion (3 months free on annual Pro) expired on June 30, 2025.

## Notes for Sales

- All quotes must use the current pricing structure document for the relevant quarter. Do not reference prior-quarter pricing documents in proposals.
- Custom discounts beyond the volume schedule require VP of Sales approval (Marcus Rivera).
- For deal sizes exceeding $500K ARR, engage Finance for custom payment terms (quarterly billing, milestone-based billing).
