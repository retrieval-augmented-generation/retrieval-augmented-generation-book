This Cookie Policy explains how Acme Corp uses cookies and similar technologies on acmecorp.com and app.acmecorp.com. It should be read alongside our Privacy Policy.

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by a website. They are used to remember preferences, track usage, and support site functionality. Similar technologies include pixels, local storage, and session storage.

2. Cookie Categories

4. Consent Mechanism

When you first visit acmecorp.com, a cookie consent banner is displayed. You may:

  • Accept All: Enable all cookie categories.
  • Reject Non-Essential: Enable only Strictly Necessary cookies. Functional, Analytics, and Marketing cookies are not set.
  • Customize: Choose which non-essential categories to enable or disable.

Strictly Necessary cookies are always active and cannot be disabled, as the site cannot function without them.

Your consent choice is stored in a cookie (acme_consent, duration: 12 months) so the banner is not shown on every visit. If the consent cookie expires or is deleted, the banner reappears.

5. How to Change Your Preferences

You can change your cookie preferences at any time:

  • Click the "Cookie Preferences" link in the footer of any page on acmecorp.com.
  • Clear cookies in your browser settings (note: this will also clear your session and require you to log in again).
  • Use browser extensions that block specific tracking cookies.

Changes take effect immediately. Cookies from categories you disable are not set on subsequent page loads, but cookies already stored on your device are not retroactively deleted. To remove existing cookies, clear them through your browser settings.

6. Do Not Track

Some browsers send a "Do Not Track" (DNT) signal. Acme Corp does not currently respond to DNT signals because there is no industry-standard interpretation. We rely on the cookie consent mechanism described in Section 4 as the primary means for users to control tracking.

7. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are set by third parties (Google, Meta, LinkedIn, Amplitude) as indicated in Section 3. These third parties may combine data collected on our site with data from other sites. Acme Corp does not control how third parties use their cookies. Refer to each provider's privacy policy for details.

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy to reflect changes in our practices or applicable law. Material changes are communicated via a refreshed consent banner. The "Last Updated" date at the top indicates the most recent revision.
