# Leadership Announcement: Jordan Patel Appointed Chief Compliance Officer

**Date:** January 6, 2025
**From:** David Kim, Chief Executive Officer
**To:** All Acme Corp Employees

---

I am pleased to announce that **Jordan Patel** has been appointed **Chief Compliance Officer** (CCO), effective immediately.

## Background

Jordan joins the executive team after 4 years at Acme Corp, most recently serving as Senior Director of Risk and Compliance. In that role, Jordan led the company through its first SOC 2 Type II audit (March 2024), managed the ISO 27001 certification renewal (January 2024), and built the compliance infrastructure that supports our HIPAA-compliant healthcare product line.

Before joining Acme Corp, Jordan spent 8 years in compliance leadership roles at two enterprise SaaS companies, including 3 years as VP of Compliance at DataShield, where they oversaw a team of 15 across audit, privacy, and regulatory affairs.

Jordan holds a JD from Georgetown University Law Center and is a Certified Information Privacy Professional (CIPP/US and CIPP/E).

## Responsibilities

As CCO, Jordan's responsibilities include:

- Overseeing Acme Corp's compliance program across all regulatory frameworks (SOC 2, ISO 27001, HIPAA, GDPR, SOX).
- Reviewing and approving all company policies for regulatory alignment.
- Serving as the primary point of contact for external auditors and regulatory bodies.
- Managing the quarterly compliance reporting process and presenting compliance metrics to the Board of Directors.
- Coordinating with the Data Protection Officer on data privacy matters.
- Leading the compliance team (currently 6 members across audit, privacy, and regulatory affairs).
- Reviewing disclosed conflicts of interest per the Code of Conduct.

## Reporting Line

Jordan reports directly to the CEO and has a standing advisory relationship with the Audit Committee of the Board of Directors. The compliance team, the internal audit function, and the ethics hotline operations report to the CCO.

## Contact

Jordan can be reached at jordan.patel@acmecorp.com. The compliance team inbox is compliance@acmecorp.com.

Please join me in congratulating Jordan on this well-deserved appointment.

— David Kim
