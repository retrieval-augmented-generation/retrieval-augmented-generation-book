# Mandatory Training Catalog

**Effective Date:** January 10, 2025
**Applies to:** All Acme Corp employees unless otherwise noted

## Overview

The following trainings are required for all employees or for specific roles as indicated. Training completion is tracked in the learning management system (LMS). Non-completion by the stated deadline may result in suspension of system access until the training is finished.

|---|---|---|---|
| Security Awareness | Annual | March 31 | 45 min |
| Harassment Prevention | Annual | February 28 | 60 min |
| Data Privacy and Handling | Annual | March 31 | 30 min |
| Code of Conduct Acknowledgment | Annual | January 31 | 15 min |
| Insider Threat Awareness | Annual | March 31 | 20 min |

## New Hire Training

All new hires must complete the following within their first 30 days:

- Security Awareness
- Harassment Prevention
- Data Privacy and Handling
- Code of Conduct Acknowledgment

Department-specific trainings must be completed within 60 days of the hire date.

## Non-Compliance

Employees who have not completed mandatory training by the stated deadline are subject to the following escalation:

1. **7 days past deadline:** Automated reminder from the LMS.
2. **14 days past deadline:** Manager notified; written warning issued.
3. **30 days past deadline:** System access suspended until training is completed.

Departments with a training completion rate below 90% at the end of a quarter are flagged in the quarterly compliance report and are required to submit a remediation plan to Human Resources within 15 business days.

## Policy Governance

Mandatory training requirements are maintained by Human Resources in collaboration with the Compliance, IT, and Legal teams. Questions should be directed to training@acmecorp.com.
