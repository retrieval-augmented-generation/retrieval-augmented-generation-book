Wellness Program Overview

Effective Date: June 1, 2024
Applies to: All full-time Acme Corp employees

1. Gym Reimbursement

Acme Corp reimburses up to $50 per month toward gym memberships or fitness classes. Eligible expenses include:

  • Monthly gym or fitness studio memberships.
  • Group fitness classes (yoga, cycling, martial arts, etc.).
  • Virtual fitness subscriptions.

Reimbursement requests are submitted through the benefits portal with a receipt or membership statement. Requests must be submitted within 60 days of the charge. Unused monthly amounts do not roll over.

2. Annual Health Screening

All full-time employees are eligible for one company-sponsored health screening per calendar year at no cost. The screening includes:

  • Biometric measurements (blood pressure, BMI, waist circumference).
  • Blood panel (cholesterol, glucose, triglycerides).
  • Confidential results report sent directly to the employee.

Screenings are offered on-site during Q1 each year. Employees who cannot attend the on-site event may complete a screening at a participating provider and submit the results for credit. Scheduling details are communicated via company-wide email in January.

3. Employee Assistance Program (EAP)

Acme Corp provides access to a confidential Employee Assistance Program through ComPsych at no cost to employees or their household members. The EAP offers:

  • Up to 6 confidential counseling sessions per issue per year (in-person or virtual).
  • 24/7 crisis support hotline.
  • Referrals for legal, financial, and work-life resources.
  • Substance abuse assessment and referral.

EAP usage is completely confidential. No information is shared with Acme Corp management or Human Resources. Contact ComPsych directly at 1-800-555-0199 or through the online portal at eap.acmecorp.com.

4. Wellness Incentives

Employees who complete all three wellness activities during the calendar year — gym reimbursement participation, annual health screening, and an EAP financial wellness webinar — receive a $200 wellness credit applied to their December paycheck.

5. Contact

The wellness program is administered by Human Resources. Questions should be directed to benefits@acmecorp.com or ext. 4200.
