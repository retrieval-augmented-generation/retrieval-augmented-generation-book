# Sick Leave Policy

**Effective Date:** January 15, 2022
**Applies to:** All full-time Acme Corp employees

## 1. Accrual

Full-time employees accrue sick leave at a rate of 1 day per month (8 hours), up to a maximum balance of 15 days (120 hours). Sick leave does not accrue beyond the 15-day cap. Unused sick leave carries over year to year but is not paid out upon separation.

Part-time employees accrue sick leave on a prorated basis relative to scheduled weekly hours.

## 2. Permitted Use

Sick leave may be used for:

- The employee's own illness, injury, or medical appointment.
- Care of an immediate family member (spouse, child, parent) with an illness or medical appointment.
- Mental health days, limited to 3 per calendar year without medical documentation.

Sick leave may not be used for vacation purposes. Patterns of misuse (e.g., repeated absences on Mondays or Fridays) may be flagged for review by the employee's manager and Human Resources.

## 3. Notification

Employees must notify their manager before the start of their scheduled shift, or as soon as practicable. Notification by email, phone, or the company's messaging system is acceptable. For planned medical appointments, employees should provide at least 2 business days' notice.

## 4. Documentation

A doctor's note is required for absences exceeding 3 consecutive days. The note must confirm the employee was seen by a provider and include an expected return-to-work date. Detailed diagnosis information is not required.

Human Resources may request documentation for shorter absences if a pattern of concern has been identified and documented by the employee's manager.

## 5. Coordination with Other Leave

- Sick leave may be used to cover the 7-day waiting period under the Short-Term Disability Policy.
- Sick leave runs concurrently with FMLA leave when applicable.
- Employees who exhaust sick leave may use accrued vacation for continued absences.

## 6. Policy Governance

This policy is administered by Human Resources. Questions should be directed to hr@acmecorp.com.
