# Jury Duty Leave Policy

**Effective Date:** June 15, 2022
**Applies to:** All Acme Corp employees (full-time and part-time)

## 1. Paid Jury Duty Leave

Employees receive full pay for up to 10 days of jury duty per year. This applies to service on any federal, state, or local jury, including grand jury duty. Days need not be consecutive.

Jury duty beyond 10 days in a calendar year is unpaid but job-protected. Employees may use accrued vacation to cover unpaid jury duty days at their discretion.

## 2. Notification and Documentation

Employees must:

1. Notify their manager and Human Resources within 2 business days of receiving a jury duty summons.
2. Provide a copy of the summons to Human Resources before the service date.
3. Submit proof of attendance (court-issued certificate or signed statement) within 3 business days of completing service for each day or block of days served.

Failure to provide documentation may result in the absence being recorded as unexcused.

## 3. Work Expectations During Jury Service

Employees are not required to work on days they report for jury duty. On days where the employee is dismissed early (before 1:00 PM local time), they should contact their manager to discuss whether returning to work for the remainder of the day is practical. There is no obligation to return, but the company appreciates the effort when feasible.

Employees are not expected to work evenings or weekends to compensate for time spent on jury duty.

## 4. Return to Work

Employees return to their regular position and schedule on the next business day after jury duty ends. No formal reinstatement process is required. If jury service exceeded 10 consecutive business days, the manager and employee should schedule a brief check-in to review any project updates or priority changes that occurred during the absence.

## 5. Retaliation Prohibited

Acme Corp prohibits any adverse action against an employee for serving on a jury. This includes termination, demotion, reduction in hours, or any form of discipline related to jury service.

## 6. Policy Governance

This policy is administered by Human Resources. Questions should be directed to hr@acmecorp.com.
