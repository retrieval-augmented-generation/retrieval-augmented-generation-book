# Q4 2025 Pricing Structure

**Effective:** October 1 – December 31, 2025
**Supersedes:** Q3 2025 Pricing Structure
**Approved by:** Lisa Yamamoto, CFO

---

## Summary of Changes from Q3

The following changes take effect on October 1, 2025:

1. **Enterprise minimum contract value** increased from $50,000 to $100,000 annually for discount eligibility.
2. **Volume discount bands** restructured — the 50–99 seat tier is eliminated; discounts now begin at 100 seats.
3. **New startup tier** introduced at a 40% discount off Pro pricing for companies with fewer than 50 employees.
4. **Compliance Suite add-on** price reduced from $12 to $10/seat/month to drive adoption.

Starter and Pro base pricing are unchanged from Q3.

## Standard Pricing

| Tier | Monthly Price (per seat) | Annual Price (per seat) | Minimum Seats |
|---|---|---|---|
| Starter | $19/month | $199/year ($16.58/month effective) | 1 |
| Pro | $59/month | $637/year ($53.08/month effective) | 5 |
| Startup (new) | $35/month | $379/year ($31.58/month effective) | 5 |
| Enterprise | Custom | Custom | 50 |

The Startup tier is available to companies with fewer than 50 employees, verified during the sales process. Companies that grow beyond 50 employees transition to Pro pricing at their next renewal.

## Volume Discounts

Volume discounts apply to Pro and Enterprise tiers. The discount schedule has been restructured for Q4:

| Committed Seats | Pro Discount | Enterprise Discount |
|---|---|---|
| 100–249 | 10% | Included in negotiation |
| 250–499 | 15% | Included in negotiation |
| 500–999 | 20% | Included in negotiation |
| 1,000+ | 25% | Included in negotiation |

The minimum contract value for Enterprise discount eligibility is $100,000 annually, increased from $50,000 in Q3. This change reflects the cost of dedicated support resources (TAM, CSM) allocated to Enterprise accounts. Contracts between $50,000 and $99,999 are served on the Pro tier with volume discounts.

**Key change from Q3:** The 50–99 seat discount tier (previously 5%) has been eliminated. Analysis showed that accounts in this band had a high support cost ratio relative to revenue. Customers with 50–99 seats on Pro are billed at the standard rate.

## Product Line Add-Ons

| Add-On | Q3 Price | Q4 Price | Change |
|---|---|---|---|
| Data & Analytics | $15/seat/month | $15/seat/month | No change |
| Compliance Suite | $12/seat/month | $10/seat/month | -$2 (price reduction) |
| Advanced API (higher rate limits) | $8/seat/month | $8/seat/month | No change |

The Compliance Suite price reduction is intended to increase attach rate. Current attach rate is 22% of Pro and Enterprise accounts; the target for Q1 2026 is 35%.

## Contract Terms

- **Starter:** Month-to-month or annual. No minimum commitment beyond 1 seat.
- **Startup (new):** Annual only. 12-month minimum commitment. 5-seat minimum.
- **Pro:** Month-to-month or annual. Annual contracts receive approximately 10% savings over monthly billing. 5-seat minimum.
- **Enterprise:** Annual or multi-year (2–3 years). Multi-year contracts receive an additional 5–10% discount. 50-seat minimum. $100,000 annual minimum for discount eligibility.

## Q4 Promotions

- **Compliance Bundle:** Customers who add the Compliance Suite to a new or renewing annual Pro contract in Q4 receive the first 3 months of the add-on free.
- **Startup Launch Offer:** First 100 companies to sign up for the Startup tier receive a $500 onboarding credit.

## Compliance Implications

The pricing restructure has compliance implications for revenue recognition:

- The new Startup tier introduces a time-limited eligibility criterion (company size < 50). Finance must verify eligibility at contract signing and at renewal.
- The Compliance Bundle promotion creates a deferred revenue component for the 3 free months. Revenue recognition follows ASC 606 guidance for contract modifications.
- Sales should consult Finance before offering non-standard terms that deviate from this document.

## Notes for Sales

- All Q4 quotes must reference this document. Q3 pricing documents are no longer valid for new proposals.
- The Startup tier is positioned as a land-and-expand play. Highlight the upgrade path to Pro as the company grows.
- For renewals of customers currently in the $50K–$99K range who were previously classified as Enterprise, prepare a transition communication. The Customer Success team has templates.
- Custom discounts beyond the volume schedule require VP of Sales approval (Marcus Rivera).
