# 401(k) Matching Policy

**Effective Date:** April 1, 2023
**Applies to:** All Acme Corp employees eligible for benefits

## 1. Employer Match

Acme Corp matches 50% of employee contributions up to 6% of salary. This results in a maximum employer contribution of 3% of salary.

**Example:** An employee earning $100,000 per year who contributes 6% ($6,000) receives a 50% match of $3,000. Contributions above 6% of salary are not matched.

| Employee Contribution (% of salary) | Employer Match (% of salary) |
|---|---|
| 1% | 0.5% |
| 2% | 1.0% |
| 3% | 1.5% |
| 4% | 2.0% |
| 5% | 2.5% |
| 6% or more | 3.0% (maximum) |

## 2. Eligibility

All benefits-eligible employees may participate in the 401(k) plan from their first day of employment. Enrollment is available through the benefits portal at any time; there is no requirement to wait for open enrollment.

Employees are automatically enrolled at a 3% contribution rate within 30 days of their start date unless they opt out or select a different rate.

## 3. Vesting Schedule

Employer matching contributions vest over a 4-year schedule:

| Years of Service | Vested Percentage |
|---|---|
| Less than 1 year | 0% |
| 1 year | 25% |
| 2 years | 50% |
| 3 years | 75% |
| 4 years | 100% |

Employee contributions are always 100% vested. Only the employer match is subject to the vesting schedule. Unvested employer contributions are forfeited upon separation from the company.

## 4. Contribution Limits

Employee contributions are subject to IRS annual limits. For 2025, the limit is $23,500 for employees under 50 and $31,000 for employees 50 and older (includes the $7,500 catch-up contribution). Limits are updated annually; Payroll will notify employees of changes each January.

Contributions may be made on a pre-tax or Roth (after-tax) basis. The employer match is always made on a pre-tax basis regardless of the employee's election.

## 5. Fund Options

The plan offers a selection of index funds, target-date funds, and a self-directed brokerage window. A full list of available funds, expense ratios, and historical performance is available on the benefits portal. Acme Corp does not provide investment advice; employees are encouraged to consult a financial advisor.

## 6. Loans and Hardship Withdrawals

Participants may borrow from their vested 401(k) balance up to the lesser of $50,000 or 50% of the vested balance. Loan terms do not exceed 5 years (or 15 years for a primary residence purchase). One outstanding loan is permitted at a time.

Hardship withdrawals are available for qualifying events as defined by IRS regulations. Hardship withdrawals are subject to income tax and may incur a 10% early withdrawal penalty for participants under 59½.

## 7. Policy Governance

The 401(k) plan is administered by Human Resources in partnership with Fidelity Investments as the plan recordkeeper. Questions should be directed to benefits@acmecorp.com or ext. 4200.
