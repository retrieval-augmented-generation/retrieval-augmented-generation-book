Onboarding Quickstart

Estimated time: 10 minutes
Last Updated: February 1, 2025

This guide walks new users through setup from account creation to your first search. Follow the steps in order.

Step 1 — Sign In

~1 minute

Open app.acmecorp.com in your browser and sign in with your company email. If your organization uses SSO (Okta, Azure AD, Google Workspace), click Sign in with SSO and enter your company domain. First-time users will be prompted to set up multi-factor authentication before proceeding.

Step 2 — Set Up Your Profile

~1 minute

Click your avatar in the top right and select Profile. Add your name, job title, and department. This information is visible to your team and helps others find the right person for collaboration.

Step 3 — Join Your Team Workspace

~1 minute

Your administrator may have already assigned you to a workspace. Check the left sidebar for your team's workspace. If you do not see one, ask your administrator to add you, or create a new workspace under Settings > Workspaces > Create.

Step 4 — Create Your First Record

~2 minutes

Click the + New Record button in the top bar. Give it a name, add some content, and optionally tag it with a category. Click Save. Your record is now searchable by everyone in your workspace.

Step 5 — Try a Search

~2 minutes

Click the search bar (or press /) and type a question or keyword. If your plan supports hybrid search (Pro and above), the system combines keyword matching with semantic understanding to find the most relevant results. Try searching for the record you just created.

Step 6 — Connect an Integration

~3 minutes

Go to Settings > Integrations to see available connections. Popular starting points:

  • Slack: Get notifications in your team channel when records are created or updated.
  • Zapier: Automate workflows between Acme Corp and 5,000+ other apps.
  • Email forwarding: Send emails to your workspace's unique ingest address to create records automatically.

See the Integrations Catalog for the full list.
