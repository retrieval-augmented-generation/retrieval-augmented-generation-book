# Warranty Terms

**Effective Date:** October 1, 2023
**Applies to:** All physical products sold or shipped by Acme Corp (hardware kits, developer devices, branded merchandise)

---

## 1. Standard Warranty

All physical products shipped by Acme Corp carry a 1-year standard warranty from the date of delivery. The standard warranty covers defects in materials and workmanship under normal use.

During the warranty period, Acme Corp will, at its discretion, repair or replace the defective product at no cost to the customer. Replacement products may be new or refurbished and carry the remainder of the original warranty period or 90 days, whichever is longer.

## 2. Extended Warranty

Customers may purchase an extended warranty at the time of order or within 30 days of delivery:

| Option | Duration | Cost |
|---|---|---|
| Extended 1-Year | 2 years total coverage | $49 |
| Extended 2-Year | 3 years total coverage | $89 |

Extended warranty terms are identical to the standard warranty. Coverage begins at the expiration of the standard warranty period.

Extended warranties are non-refundable once the standard warranty period has expired.

## 3. Claim Process

To file a warranty claim:

1. Contact operations@acmecorp.com with your order number, a description of the defect, and photos if applicable.
2. The Operations team reviews the claim and responds within 3 business days.
3. If approved, Acme Corp provides a prepaid shipping label for the defective product.
4. Upon receipt and inspection, a replacement is shipped within 5 business days.

For products under $50 in value, Acme Corp may ship a replacement without requiring return of the defective item.

## 4. What Is NOT Covered

The warranty does not cover:

- Damage from misuse, abuse, accident, or neglect.
- Damage from unauthorized modification or repair.
- Normal wear and tear, including cosmetic damage (scratches, dents) that does not affect functionality.
- Damage caused by use with non-compatible third-party accessories or power supplies.
- Products with removed or altered serial numbers.
- Software or firmware issues (covered under the applicable software license agreement, not this warranty).
- Consumable components (batteries, cables, stylus tips) after the first 90 days.
- Damage from exposure to liquids, extreme temperatures, or corrosive environments.
- Products purchased from unauthorized resellers.

## 5. Interaction with Refund Policy

The warranty and the Refund Policy operate independently:

- A subscription refund (governed by the Refund Policy) does not void an active warranty on physical products.
- A warranty replacement does not extend or restart the 30-day refund window for the subscription.
- Customers may have an active warranty claim and a subscription refund request at the same time. Each is processed under its own policy.

If a product is defective on arrival, the customer may choose either a warranty replacement (this document) or a full product refund under the Refund Policy's 30-day window. Both options cannot be exercised for the same item.

## 6. Limitation of Liability

Acme Corp's liability under this warranty is limited to the repair or replacement of the defective product. Acme Corp is not liable for indirect, incidental, or consequential damages arising from product defects, including but not limited to lost data, lost revenue, or costs of substitute equipment.
