# Bereavement Leave Policy

**Effective Date:** April 1, 2021
**Applies to:** All Acme Corp employees (full-time and part-time)

## 1. Paid Bereavement Leave

Employees are granted paid bereavement leave following the death of a family member:

| Relationship | Paid Days |
|---|---|
| Immediate family (spouse, domestic partner, child, parent, sibling) | Up to 5 days |
| Extended family (grandparent, grandchild, in-law, aunt, uncle, niece, nephew) | Up to 3 days |

Leave days need not be consecutive but must be taken within 30 calendar days of the date of death or memorial service.

## 2. Additional Unpaid Leave

Employees who need time beyond the paid allotment may request additional unpaid bereavement leave of up to 5 business days. Requests are submitted to the employee's manager and Human Resources. Approval is at manager discretion based on individual circumstances.

Employees may also use accrued vacation or sick leave to extend their paid absence.

## 3. Notification

Employees should notify their manager as soon as practicable. Human Resources may request documentation (e.g., obituary, funeral program, or death certificate) for absences exceeding 3 days.

## 4. Travel Considerations

Employees who need to travel a significant distance (more than 500 miles one way) for funeral arrangements or services may request 1 additional paid travel day per trip, subject to manager approval.

## 5. Policy Governance

This policy is administered by Human Resources. Questions should be directed to hr@acmecorp.com.
