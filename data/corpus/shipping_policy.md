# Shipping Policy

**Effective Date:** October 1, 2023
**Applies to:** All physical product orders (hardware kits, welcome packages, branded merchandise) shipped by Acme Corp

---

## 1. Standard Shipping

Standard shipping takes 5–7 business days from the date the order is processed. Processing typically occurs within 1 business day of order confirmation. Standard shipping is provided at no cost for all domestic (U.S.) orders.

Tracking information is sent via email once the shipment is dispatched.

## 2. Expedited Shipping

| Option | Delivery Time | Cost |
|---|---|---|
| 2-Day | 2 business days from processing | $15 per shipment |
| Overnight | Next business day by 3:00 PM local time | $35 per shipment |

Expedited orders placed before 12:00 PM PT are processed the same day. Orders placed after 12:00 PM PT are processed the next business day.

Saturday and Sunday deliveries are not available for expedited shipments.

## 3. International Shipping

Acme Corp ships to the following regions:

- **Canada:** 7–12 business days. Flat rate $20.
- **European Union:** 10–15 business days. Flat rate $35.
- **United Kingdom:** 10–15 business days. Flat rate $35.
- **Australia / New Zealand:** 12–18 business days. Flat rate $45.

International shipments may be subject to customs duties, import taxes, and brokerage fees assessed by the destination country. These charges are the responsibility of the recipient and are not included in the shipping cost.

Acme Corp does not currently ship to countries subject to U.S. trade sanctions.

## 4. Order Tracking

All shipments include tracking. Tracking numbers are emailed to the recipient within 24 hours of dispatch. Tracking is available at the carrier's website (UPS for domestic, DHL for international).

## 5. Lost or Damaged Shipments

If a shipment is lost or arrives damaged:

1. Contact operations@acmecorp.com within 14 days of the expected delivery date.
2. Provide the order number and a description (and photos, if damaged).
3. Acme Corp will file a carrier claim and ship a replacement at no cost within 3 business days of the report.

## 6. Policy Governance

This policy is maintained by the Operations team. Questions should be directed to operations@acmecorp.com.
