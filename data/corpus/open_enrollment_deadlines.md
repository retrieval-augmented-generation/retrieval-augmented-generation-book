# Open Enrollment Deadlines

**Plan Year:** 2025
**Applies to:** All benefits-eligible Acme Corp employees

## 1. Enrollment Window

The annual open enrollment period runs from November 1 through November 15. All benefit elections for the upcoming plan year must be submitted by 11:59 PM PT on November 15. Elections take effect January 1.

Employees who do not make changes during open enrollment will be automatically re-enrolled in their current plan selections. HSA and FSA contributions do not carry forward automatically and must be re-elected each year.

## 2. What You Can Change

During open enrollment, employees may:

- Enroll in, change, or waive health insurance (Basic, Standard, or Premium).
- Add or remove dependents from health, dental, or vision coverage.
- Enroll in or change supplemental life insurance coverage.
- Set or adjust HSA contributions (Basic and Standard plan enrollees only).
- Set or adjust Healthcare FSA or Dependent Care FSA contributions.
- Enroll in or cancel the standalone dental or vision plan (for employees not on the Premium plan).

## 3. Mid-Year Changes

Outside of the open enrollment window, benefit elections may only be changed within 30 days of a qualifying life event:

- Marriage or divorce.
- Birth, adoption, or foster placement of a child.
- Death of a dependent.
- Loss of coverage under a spouse's or parent's plan.
- Change in dependent eligibility (e.g., dependent aging out at 26).
- Gain or loss of Medicaid or CHIP eligibility.

Employees must submit a qualifying life event change request through the benefits portal within 30 days of the event. Supporting documentation (marriage certificate, birth certificate, court order, or letter of coverage loss) is required.

## 4. New Hire Enrollment

New employees have 30 days from their start date to make initial benefit elections. If no elections are made within 30 days, the employee is enrolled in the Basic health plan with employee-only coverage and no FSA or supplemental life insurance. 401(k) auto-enrollment at 3% proceeds independently per the 401(k) Matching Policy.

## 5. Key Dates

| Date | Event |
|---|---|
| October 1 | Benefits fair and plan information published |
| November 1 | Open enrollment opens |
| November 15 | Open enrollment closes (11:59 PM PT) |
| December | Confirmation statements distributed |
| January 1 | New elections take effect |
