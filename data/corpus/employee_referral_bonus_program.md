# Employee Referral Bonus Program

**Effective Date:** January 15, 2024
**Applies to:** All full-time Acme Corp employees who have completed their 90-day probationary period

## 1. Overview

Acme Corp encourages employees to refer qualified candidates for open positions. Successful referrals that result in a hire are eligible for a $2,000 referral bonus.

## 2. Eligibility Requirements

To qualify for the referral bonus, all of the following conditions must be met:

1. **Referral form submitted before the candidate applies.** The referring employee must submit a referral through the HR portal or by emailing recruiting@acmecorp.com before the candidate submits their application. Retroactive referrals (submitted after the candidate has already applied) are not eligible.
2. **The candidate is hired** into a full-time position at Acme Corp.
3. **The candidate must remain employed for at least 90 days** from their start date. If the candidate voluntarily resigns or is terminated for cause before the 90-day mark, the bonus is forfeited.
4. **The referring employee must be employed at Acme Corp** at the time the bonus is paid. Employees who resign or are terminated before the bonus payment date forfeit the bonus.

## 3. Bonus Payment

The $2,000 referral bonus is paid in the next regular pay cycle following the referred candidate's 90th day of employment. The bonus is subject to standard payroll taxes and withholding.

## 4. Referral Limits

Each employee may submit a maximum of 5 referrals per calendar year. This limit applies to referrals submitted, not hires made. If an employee has submitted 5 referral forms in a given year and none resulted in a hire, no additional referrals may be submitted until the following January 1.

## 5. Exclusions

The following are not eligible for referral bonuses:

- Referrals made by members of the hiring team for the specific role (hiring manager, interviewers on the loop, recruiting team).
- Referrals of former Acme Corp employees who separated within the prior 12 months.
- Referrals for temporary, intern, or contractor positions.
- Referrals submitted by employees still in their 90-day probationary period.

## 6. Multiple Referrals for the Same Candidate

If more than one employee refers the same candidate, the bonus is awarded to the employee whose referral was submitted first, based on the timestamp in the HR system. Duplicate referrals are not split.

## 7. Policy Governance

This program is administered by the Talent Acquisition team within Human Resources. Questions should be directed to recruiting@acmecorp.com.
