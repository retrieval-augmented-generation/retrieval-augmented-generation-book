# Expense Report Approval Policy

**Effective Date:** November 1, 2020
**Applies to:** All Acme Corp employees

## 1. Approval Thresholds

Expense reports require the following approvals based on the total amount:

| Expense Amount | Required Approval |
|---|---|
| Under $500 | Direct manager |
| $500 – $10,000 | Direct manager and department head |
| Over $10,000 | Direct manager, department head, and CFO |

All approvals must be completed in the expense management system before reimbursement is processed. Verbal approvals are not accepted.

## 2. Eligible Expenses

Reimbursable business expenses include:

- Business travel (airfare, lodging, ground transportation, meals).
- Client entertainment and meals (with business purpose documented).
- Conference and training registration fees.
- Professional development materials (books, subscriptions, courses).
- Office supplies not available through the standard procurement process.
- Home office equipment covered under the Remote Work Policy stipend.

## 3. Non-Reimbursable Expenses

The following are not eligible for reimbursement:

- Personal expenses of any kind.
- Alcohol (except at approved client entertainment events, limited to 2 drinks per person).
- First-class or business-class airfare (economy or premium economy only, unless the flight exceeds 6 hours).
- Hotel room upgrades beyond the company's negotiated rate.
- Spouse or family travel costs.
- Traffic violations or parking tickets.

## 4. Submission Requirements

- Expense reports must be submitted within 30 days of the expense being incurred.
- Receipts are required for all individual expenses over $25.
- Each line item must include a business purpose description.
- International expenses must be converted to USD using the exchange rate on the date of the transaction.

Reports submitted more than 60 days after the expense was incurred require VP-level approval and may be denied.

## 5. Reimbursement Timeline

Approved expense reports are reimbursed via direct deposit within 10 business days of final approval. If an approver is out of office, the employee may request delegation to an alternate approver through the HR portal.

## 6. Corporate Credit Cards

Employees at L5 and above are eligible for a corporate credit card. Corporate card expenses follow the same approval thresholds and submission requirements as out-of-pocket expenses. Unreconciled corporate card transactions older than 45 days are flagged by Finance and escalated to the employee's manager.

## 7. Policy Governance

This policy is administered by the Finance department. Questions should be directed to finance@acmecorp.com.
