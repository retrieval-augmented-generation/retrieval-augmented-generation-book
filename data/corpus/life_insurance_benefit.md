# Life Insurance Benefit

**Effective Date:** August 1, 2021
**Applies to:** All full-time Acme Corp employees

## 1. Basic Life Insurance

Acme Corp provides basic life insurance coverage equal to 1x the employee's annual base salary, at no cost to the employee. Coverage is effective on the first day of employment and remains in effect for the duration of employment.

The benefit maximum under the basic plan is $500,000. Employees whose base salary exceeds $500,000 are capped at that amount for basic coverage.

Basic life insurance includes accidental death and dismemberment (AD&D) coverage at the same level.

## 2. Supplemental Life Insurance

Employees may purchase additional voluntary life insurance through payroll deduction:

| Coverage Level | Evidence of Insurability Required |
|---|---|
| Up to 2x salary | No (guaranteed issue at initial enrollment) |
| 3x–5x salary | Yes (medical questionnaire required) |

Guaranteed issue is available only during the employee's initial enrollment window (within 30 days of hire) or during annual open enrollment for employees who have not previously enrolled. Employees requesting coverage above 2x salary at any time must complete the evidence of insurability process.

The supplemental benefit maximum is 5x annual base salary, capped at $1,500,000.

## 3. Spousal and Dependent Coverage

Employees may also purchase life insurance for:

- **Spouse/domestic partner:** Flat amounts of $25,000, $50,000, or $100,000. Evidence of insurability is required for amounts above $25,000.
- **Dependent children (age 14 days to 26 years):** $10,000 flat benefit. No evidence of insurability required.

## 4. Beneficiary Designation

Employees must designate a primary and contingent beneficiary through the benefits portal. Beneficiary designations may be updated at any time — there is no requirement to wait for open enrollment.

If no beneficiary designation is on file at the time of death, the benefit is paid in the following order: surviving spouse, surviving children in equal shares, the employee's estate.

Employees are encouraged to review their beneficiary designations annually and after major life events (marriage, divorce, birth of a child).

## 5. Portability

Employees who separate from Acme Corp may convert their basic and supplemental life insurance to an individual policy within 31 days of their last day of employment. Conversion rates are set by the insurer and are typically higher than the group rate. Details are provided in the separation packet.

## 6. Policy Governance

Life insurance benefits are administered by Human Resources in partnership with MetLife as the group carrier. Questions should be directed to benefits@acmecorp.com or ext. 4200.
