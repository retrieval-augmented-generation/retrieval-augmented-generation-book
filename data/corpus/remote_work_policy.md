# Remote Work Policy

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2024
**Last Updated:** September 12, 2025
**Applies to:** All full-time Acme Corp employees

## 1. Eligibility

Remote and hybrid work arrangements are available to full-time employees with manager approval. Eligibility is based on role suitability, performance history, and team needs. Employees in their 90-day probationary period may work remotely only with written approval from their manager and HR.

This policy applies to Acme Corp employees only. Contractors, temporary workers, and interns are not covered by this policy and should refer to the terms of their contracting agreement or the Contractor Access Policy.

## 2. In-Office Requirements

Approved remote employees must work from the office a minimum of 2 days per week. The specific days may be set by the employee's manager based on team collaboration needs. Managers may designate required in-office days for their team (e.g., Tuesdays and Thursdays).

Exceptions to the 2-day minimum require written approval from the department head and Human Resources. Exceptions are reviewed quarterly.

## 3. Core Hours

All employees, whether working on-site or remotely, must be available during core hours of 10:00 AM to 3:00 PM in their local time zone. "Available" means reachable via company messaging (Slack), email, or video call within a reasonable response time (generally 30 minutes).

Employees working across time zones should coordinate with their manager to establish overlapping availability windows.

## 4. Home Office Setup Stipend

Employees approved for remote work receive a one-time home office setup stipend of $500. The stipend is intended for ergonomic furniture, monitors, peripherals, or other equipment needed to maintain a productive workspace.

The stipend is paid through payroll reimbursement upon submission of receipts. Receipts must be submitted within 90 days of remote work approval. The stipend is not available to employees who subsequently return to full-time in-office work and then re-apply for remote status.

## 5. Workspace Requirements

Remote employees must maintain a workspace that meets the following requirements:

- Reliable internet connection (minimum 25 Mbps download / 5 Mbps upload).
- A private space suitable for video calls and handling confidential information.
- Compliance with the Laptop Security Requirements (full disk encryption, endpoint agent installed, screen lock enabled).

Acme Corp is not responsible for the employee's internet service, utilities, or home office maintenance costs beyond the one-time stipend.

## 6. Data Security

Remote employees must comply with all Acme Corp security policies, including the IT Security Policy and the Data Classification Policy. Specifically:

- Company data must not be accessed on personal devices unless those devices are enrolled in the company's MDM (Mobile Device Management) program per the BYOD Policy.
- VPN must be used when accessing internal systems from outside the corporate network.
- Confidential and Restricted data must not be printed or stored locally outside of encrypted company-managed devices.

## 7. Performance Expectations

Remote work does not change performance expectations. Remote employees are held to the same objectives, deadlines, and quality standards as on-site employees. Managers should address any performance concerns through the standard quarterly check-in and performance review process.

If a remote employee's performance declines and the manager determines that the remote arrangement is a contributing factor, the manager may require a return to full-time in-office work with 2 weeks' notice, subject to HR review.

## 8. Revocation

Remote work approval may be revoked at any time for business reasons, performance concerns, or policy violations. Employees will receive at least 2 weeks' notice before a required return to full-time in-office work, except in cases of policy violations where immediate revocation may apply.

## 9. Policy Governance

This policy is administered by Human Resources. Questions should be directed to hr@acmecorp.com. This policy was last updated on September 12, 2025.
