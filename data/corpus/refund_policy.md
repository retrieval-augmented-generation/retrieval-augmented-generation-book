# Refund Policy

**Effective Date:** March 1, 2024
**Applies to:** All Acme Corp self-serve and Pro tier customers purchasing through the online store or in-app checkout

This policy covers consumer and self-serve refund requests. Enterprise customers should refer to the Enterprise Refund Policy.

---

## 1. Overview

Acme Corp offers refunds on subscription fees for online purchases within the window described below. Refunds are issued to the original payment method and typically appear within 5–7 business days of approval.

## 2. Refund Terms

### 2.1 Online Purchases

The maximum refund period for online purchases is **30 days from the date of delivery** — defined as the date the customer's account is provisioned and access is confirmed. Within this 30-day window:

- Customers may request a full refund for any reason.
- No cancellation fee applies.
- Refunds are processed within 10 business days of the approved request.

After 30 days, subscription fees are non-refundable. Customers may cancel at any time to prevent future charges, but no refund is issued for the current billing period.

### 2.2 Exclusions

The following are not eligible for refund under this policy:

- **Add-on purchases** (Data & Analytics, Compliance Suite, Advanced API) are non-refundable once activated, regardless of the time elapsed. Customers are advised to use the 14-day free trial for add-ons before committing.
- **Usage-based overages** (API calls, storage, data export bandwidth) that have already been consumed.
- **Annual prepayments** after 30 days. Annual subscribers who cancel after the 30-day window forfeit the prepaid balance for the remainder of the term.
- **Promotional credits and discounts.** If the original purchase included a promotional discount, the refund is calculated on the amount actually paid, not the list price.
- **Professional services and training** purchased separately from the subscription.

### 2.3 Partial Refunds

Partial refunds are not available for monthly subscriptions. For annual subscriptions cancelled within the 30-day window, the full annual prepayment is refunded. There is no prorated partial refund after the 30-day window.

## 3. How to Request a Refund

1. Log in to your account at app.acmecorp.com.
2. Navigate to **Billing > Subscription > Request Refund**.
3. Select the reason for the refund from the dropdown menu.
4. Confirm the request.

Alternatively, email billing-support@acmecorp.com with your account ID and a brief description of the reason.

Acme Corp reviews all refund requests within 2 business days. If additional information is needed, the billing team will contact the customer by email.

## 4. Chargebacks

Customers are encouraged to request refunds through the process above before initiating a chargeback with their payment provider. Chargebacks incur processing fees and may result in account suspension while the dispute is reviewed.

## 5. Interaction with Warranty Terms

This refund policy covers subscription fees only. Physical products (hardware kits, branded merchandise) shipped by Acme Corp are covered separately under the Warranty Terms document. If a customer is requesting both a subscription refund and a product return, each is handled under its respective policy:

- Subscription refund: this policy (30-day window from delivery).
- Product warranty claim: the Warranty Terms (1-year standard warranty, claim process defined therein).

A subscription refund does not affect an active warranty claim, and a warranty replacement does not extend or restart the 30-day refund window for the subscription.

## 6. Policy Governance

This policy is administered by the Finance department. Questions should be directed to billing-support@acmecorp.com.
