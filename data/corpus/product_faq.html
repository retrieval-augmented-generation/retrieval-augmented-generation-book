Plans and Pricing

What plans does Acme Corp offer?

Acme Corp offers four plans:

PlanPriceBest For
Starter$19/seat/monthSmall teams getting started
Startup$35/seat/monthCompanies with fewer than 50 employees (40% discount off Pro)
Pro$59/seat/monthGrowing teams that need analytics and integrations
EnterpriseCustom pricingOrganizations requiring dedicated support, SLA guarantees, and custom configuration

Annual billing is available for all plans and offers approximately 10% savings over monthly billing. See our pricing page for current details.

Why did the Pro tier price change?

Effective November 1, 2024, the Pro tier price increased from $49/seat/month to $59/seat/month. This was the first price adjustment since the Pro tier launched in 2021 and reflects the significant features added since then, including ML-powered analytics, the Compliance Suite integration, custom reporting, and priority support. Existing annual contracts are honored at the original rate until renewal.

Can I switch plans mid-contract?

Upgrading: You can upgrade your plan at any time. The new rate is prorated for the remainder of your current billing period. If you're on an annual plan, the difference is charged immediately and your renewal date stays the same.

Downgrading: Downgrades take effect at the start of your next billing cycle. Features available only on the higher plan will stop working at that time. If you're on an annual plan, the downgrade takes effect at renewal.

Do you offer discounts for nonprofits or educational institutions?

Yes. Qualifying nonprofits and accredited educational institutions receive a 25% discount on Pro and Enterprise plans. Contact sales@acmecorp.com with proof of status to apply.

Billing

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and ACH bank transfers. Enterprise customers may also pay by wire transfer or purchase order. All payments are processed securely through our PCI-compliant payment processor — Acme Corp does not store your credit card information.

When am I billed?

Monthly plans: Billed on the same date each month (the anniversary of your signup date). If the date does not exist in a given month (e.g., the 31st), you are billed on the last day of the month.

Annual plans: Billed in full at the start of the contract. Renewal billing occurs on the anniversary of the original contract date.

How do I get an invoice?

Invoices are generated automatically after each payment and are available in your account under Billing > Invoices. You can also set up automatic invoice delivery to your finance team's email address in Billing > Settings.

What happens if my payment fails?

If a payment fails, we retry the charge after 3 days and again after 7 days. You will receive email notifications at each attempt. If all three attempts fail, your account enters a 14-day grace period during which you can update your payment method. After the grace period, your account is downgraded to read-only mode until the balance is settled.

Refunds

What is the refund policy?

The refund policy depends on your plan type:

  • Self-serve and Pro customers (online purchases): You may request a full refund within 30 days of the date your account was provisioned. After 30 days, subscription fees are non-refundable. You can cancel at any time to prevent future charges.
  • Enterprise customers: Enterprise customers may request a full refund within 45 days of purchase. After 45 days, refunds are prorated based on the remaining contract term. See the Enterprise Refund Policy for details.

To request a refund, go to Billing > Subscription > Request Refund in your account, or email billing-support@acmecorp.com.

Are add-ons refundable?

Add-ons (Data & Analytics, Compliance Suite, Advanced API) are non-refundable once activated. We offer a 14-day free trial for each add-on so you can evaluate it before committing.

Account Management

How do I add or remove users?

Account administrators can manage users under Settings > Team. Adding a user adjusts your bill at the per-seat rate for your plan. Removing a user takes effect at the end of the current billing period — no prorated credit is issued for partial months.

How do I change my account owner or administrator?

The current account owner can transfer ownership under Settings > Account > Transfer Ownership. The new owner must be an existing user on the account. If the current owner is unavailable (e.g., they have left the organization), contact support@acmecorp.com with proof of authorization from your organization.

Can I export my data?

Yes. You can export your data at any time under Settings > Data > Export. Exports are available in JSON and CSV format and typically complete within a few minutes for standard accounts. For large accounts (over 10 GB), exports are queued and you will receive an email when the download is ready.

How do I delete my account?

To delete your account, go to Settings > Account > Delete Account. Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All data is deleted within 90 days per our data retention policy. If you have an active subscription, cancel it first to avoid being charged after deletion.

Password and Security

How do I reset my password?

Go to sso.acmecorp.com/reset and enter your email address. You will be prompted to verify your identity using multi-factor authentication, then you can create a new password. Passwords must be at least 12 characters and include a mix of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and special characters. See the Self-Service Password Reset guide for detailed steps.

How do I set up multi-factor authentication (MFA)?

MFA is required for all Acme Corp accounts. To enroll, go to sso.acmecorp.com/settings/mfa and add at least two authentication methods (hardware key, authenticator app, or push notification). SMS is not supported. See the MFA Enrollment Guide for step-by-step instructions.

What do I do if I'm locked out of my account?

If you have a recovery code, use it at the login page. If all your MFA methods and recovery codes are unavailable, contact support@acmecorp.com for identity-verified account recovery. This process requires a video call with a government-issued ID.

Support

How do I contact support?

Support is available through the following channels:

  • Support portal: support.acmecorp.com (24/7 ticket submission)
  • Email: support@acmecorp.com
  • In-app chat: Available during business hours (9 AM – 6 PM PT, Monday–Friday) for Pro and Enterprise customers

Enterprise customers also have access to a dedicated phone line and Slack Connect channel. See your Enterprise SLA for response time commitments.

What are the support hours?

Standard support hours are Monday–Friday, 9:00 AM – 6:00 PM Pacific Time. Enterprise customers with a 24/7 support add-on receive around-the-clock coverage for SEV1 and SEV2 issues.

Data and Privacy

Where is my data stored?

By default, data is stored in our US-East region. Enterprise customers may request EU data residency (EU-West region) at no additional cost. The data residency region is specified in your Enterprise Agreement. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

Is Acme Corp SOC 2 certified?

Yes. Acme Corp holds SOC 2 Type II, ISO 27001, and HIPAA certifications. Our most recent SOC 2 audit was completed in March 2024 with zero critical findings. The SOC 2 report is available to customers and prospects under NDA — contact security@acmecorp.com to request a copy.

How do I submit a data subject access request (DSAR)?

Email privacy@acmecorp.com with the subject line "Data Subject Access Request." Include your full name and the email address associated with your account. We respond to all requests within 30 days.
