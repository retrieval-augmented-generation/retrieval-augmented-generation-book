How to Return an Item

Last Updated: March 1, 2024
Applies to: Physical products ordered through the Acme Corp online store

1. Return Window

You may return any physical product within 30 days of delivery. The return window is measured from the delivery date confirmed by the carrier's tracking system.

Items must be in their original packaging and in resalable condition. Opened consumables (cable packs, stylus refills) cannot be returned unless defective.

2. How to Start a Return

  1. Log in to your account at app.acmecorp.com.
  2. Go to Orders > Order History and find the order you want to return.
  3. Click Return Item next to the product.
  4. Select a return reason from the dropdown menu.
  5. Confirm the return request.

You will receive a confirmation email within 1 hour with your return authorization number (RA#).

3. Shipping Your Return

After your return is authorized:

  1. A prepaid return shipping label is included in your confirmation email. Print the label and affix it to the outside of the package.
  2. Drop off the package at any UPS location. No additional postage is required.
  3. Retain the UPS drop-off receipt as proof of shipment.
Note: If you no longer have the original packaging, use any sturdy box and include your RA# on a slip of paper inside the package. Acme Corp does not charge a restocking fee.

4. When You Will Get Your Money Back

Once the returned item is received and inspected at our warehouse, the timeline is:

StepTimeline
Warehouse receives and inspects the return1–3 business days after delivery
Return approved and credit issuedWithin 2 business days of inspection
Credit appears on your statement5–7 business days after credit is issued

Total time from drop-off to credit on your statement is typically 10–14 business days. You will receive an email when the credit is issued.

Credits are returned to the original payment method. If the original card is no longer active, contact billing-support@acmecorp.com to arrange an alternative.

5. Exchanges

Acme Corp does not offer direct exchanges. To get a different item, return the original product for a credit and place a new order.

6. Items That Cannot Be Returned

  • Products returned after the 30-day return window.
  • Items that have been physically damaged by the customer.
  • Opened consumable products (unless defective on arrival).
  • Gift cards and promotional credits.

7. Defective Items

If your item arrived defective or damaged in transit, you may choose either:

  • A return and full credit under this return policy, or
  • A warranty replacement under the Warranty Terms.

For defective items, Acme Corp covers all return shipping costs regardless of whether the item is within the 30-day return window.

8. Contact

For return questions, contact operations@acmecorp.com or call our support line at 1-800-555-0100.
