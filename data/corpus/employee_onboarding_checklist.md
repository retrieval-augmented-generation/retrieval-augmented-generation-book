# Employee Onboarding Checklist

**Effective Date:** May 1, 2024
**Applies to:** All new Acme Corp employees and their managers

## First Day

- [ ] Badge and building access provisioned by Facilities.
- [ ] Laptop and peripherals issued by IT (see Laptop Security Requirements).
- [ ] HR orientation session: company overview, benefits enrollment, policy acknowledgment.
- [ ] Manager welcome meeting: team introductions, initial project overview, and first-week expectations.
- [ ] Complete required compliance training modules (harassment prevention, security awareness, data handling).
- [ ] Enroll in benefits within 30 days via the benefits portal (default: Basic health plan if no selection is made).
- [ ] Set up direct deposit and emergency contact information in the HR system.

## 30-Day Milestone

- [ ] Complete all assigned onboarding training modules in the learning management system.
- [ ] Manager conducts a 30-day check-in to discuss initial impressions, early feedback, and any support needs.
- [ ] Employee completes a 30-day self-reflection (informal, shared with manager).
- [ ] Access to all required systems and tools confirmed by IT and manager.
- [ ] Introduction to cross-functional stakeholders and key collaborators completed.

## 60-Day Milestone

- [ ] Manager conducts a 60-day check-in to assess progress on initial objectives and provide feedback.
- [ ] Employee identifies one development goal for the remainder of the probationary period.
- [ ] Any concerns about fit, performance, or expectations are documented by the manager and shared with HR.

## 90-Day Probationary Review

All new hires enter a 90-day probationary period. The probationary review is a formal evaluation that determines whether the employee transitions to regular employment status.

- [ ] Manager completes the probationary review form (available in the HR system) by the employee's 85th day.
- [ ] Manager and employee hold a probationary review meeting before day 90.
- [ ] Review covers: performance against initial objectives, cultural fit, collaboration feedback from peers, and any areas of concern.
- [ ] HR reviews the completed form and confirms the outcome:
  - **Pass:** Employee transitions to regular status. Eligible for internal transfers, tuition reimbursement, and the employee referral bonus program.
  - **Extended probation:** An additional 30 days with specific improvement targets. Requires HR approval.
  - **Separation:** Employment ends. Coordinated through HR per the Termination Procedures.

## Post-Probation

- [ ] Employee is added to the quarterly check-in schedule per the Performance Review Process.
- [ ] Manager sets annual objectives aligned with team and department goals.
- [ ] Employee gains access to the tuition reimbursement program and employee referral bonus program.
