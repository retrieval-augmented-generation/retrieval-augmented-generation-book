VPN Access Guide

Last Updated: April 1, 2024
Applies to: All Acme Corp employees and authorized contractors working remotely

1. Overview

All remote access to Acme Corp internal systems requires the company VPN. The VPN encrypts your connection and enforces security checks (MFA, device posture) before granting access to internal resources.

3. Enrollment Steps

  1. Open your web browser and navigate to vpn.acmecorp.com/enroll.
  2. Sign in with your Acme Corp SSO credentials (your @acmecorp.com email and password).
  3. Complete the MFA challenge (hardware key or authenticator app).
  4. Download and install the VPN client for your operating system from the enrollment page.
  5. Open the VPN client and sign in when prompted. The client will register your device automatically.
  6. The client performs a device posture check: OS version, disk encryption status, and endpoint agent. If any check fails, you will see a specific error message with remediation steps.
  7. Once all checks pass, the VPN connects. A shield icon in your system tray confirms an active connection.

4. Daily Use

The VPN client runs automatically at startup and connects when it detects you are outside the corporate network. You do not need to manually connect each day.

To verify your connection status, click the VPN icon in your system tray. A green shield means connected; a gray shield means disconnected.

Split tunneling is disabled for connections to production and management zones. Personal internet traffic (streaming, personal email) routes outside the VPN tunnel when accessing non-corporate destinations.
